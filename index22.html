About Us

Cozy Café Aurora is a family-run small business serving freshly brewed coffee and homemade pastries since 2015. We believe in creating a warm, welcoming space for our community to relax, study, or catch up with friends. Our beans are sourced locally and baked goods are made fresh daily to ensure the highest quality and flavor.
