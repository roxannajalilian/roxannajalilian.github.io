About Us

Chocolate & Company is a small, family-run confectionery in Aurora, Ontario. We pride ourselves on using only the finest ingredients and making everything by hand, from chocolate truffles to plant-based gelatos.

Our mission is to delight every customer with sweet creations made with care, quality, and love. We avoid artificial flavors and colors, so you can enjoy pure, delicious treats every time.
